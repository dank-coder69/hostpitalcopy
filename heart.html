Heart Transplant

Heart Transplant is one of the major surgeries which involves removal of the patient's diseased or unhealthy heart and then its replacement with the healthy heart of a deceased donor. Fortis brings together a multidisciplinary and distinguished team of heart transplant specialists which comprises of skilled cardiac surgeons, cardiologists, anesthesiologists, critical care Intensivists, Interventional Radiologists and well trained technical and nursing staff along with some other related healthcare professionals. The transplant department endeavors to render outstanding patient care, education programs and conducting advanced research. The surgeons use innovative and highly advanced technologies to treat the patients successfully with diseases like heart failure which can occur due to various reasons such as Coronary artery disease, congenital heart defect, cardiomyopathy, Heart valve disease, etc or any other complex heart associated problems requiring a transplant.
